How we measure

One honest number, named correctly

Distribution accuracy = 1 − TV

If humans answer 70% A / 30% B and the synthetic audience answers 60% / 40%, then 10% of responses are misallocated → 90% accuracy. This is the US-census index of dissimilarity, and the exact metric Artificial Societies reports.

What we claim, and what we don't

Methodology in plain terms

  • Ground truthAggregate toplines from ANES 2024 (US), CGSS 2023 (China), Pew Global 2025 (UK). No microdata redistributed.
  • EstimandGroup distribution fidelity + response reliability. We do NOT claim to predict a specific individual's answer — a different, harder problem.
  • CeilingEvery score is shown against the sampling-noise limit of re-running the same survey (~91%).
  • ExclusionsOnly non-opinion items, degenerate items, and a documented sensitive list — never a score filter.
